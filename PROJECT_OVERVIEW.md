# 🛡️ Scope-Shield: Executive Project Overview

## Mission Statement
Scope-Shield is dedicated to protecting the professional livelihoods of freelancers and agencies through AI-driven contract intelligence. We transform complex legal documents into actionable insights, ensuring financial transparency and operational clarity.

## Core Pillars

### 1. Intelligence
Leveraging advanced AI (Google Gemini) to deconstruct legal jargon and identify potential risks before they impact your business.

### 2. Operational Clarity
Automatically generating project roadmaps and payment milestones from contract terms, providing a clear path from signing to final delivery.

### 3. Financial Security
Mitigating "scope creep" and payment delays by highlighting aggressive terms and providing tools for professional negotiation.

---

## Strategic Impact
Scope-Shield empowers professionals to:
- **Reduce Legal Risk:** Identify high-severity clauses (e.g., unlimited revisions, delayed net-90 payments) instantly.
- **Improve Profitability:** Ensure every deliverable is tracked and every milestone is billed accurately.
- **Enhance Professionalism:** Use AI-generated counter-clauses and professional project timelines to stand out to clients.

## High-Level Workflow
1. **Analyze:** Securely upload your contract PDF for comprehensive AI scanning.
2. **Review:** Visualize your risk score and explore a detailed breakdown of findings.
3. **Execute:** Track progress via an automated roadmap and manage professional billing milestones.
